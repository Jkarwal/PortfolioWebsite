About

My name is Joey Karwal and I'm a story boarder and character designer at California Institute of the Arts (CalArts).

Born in Monterey, California, Joey has lived around the U.S. before finally settling into Valencia to attend the Character Animation program at CalArts.

Since starting in the Character Animation program, he's also gone on exchange to France, Gobelins, L'Ecole d'Image during his third year spring semester.
